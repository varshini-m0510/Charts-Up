0 - 6 years

Health Care

Child Care

Food

Birth

  • BCG, Hep B1, OPV

BCG (Bacillus Calmette-Guérin): Protects against tuberculosis, especially severe forms like TB meningitis and miliary TB.

Hep B1 (Hepatitis B Vaccine 1st dose): Prevents hepatitis B, a serious liver infection caused by the hepatitis B virus.

OPV (Oral Polio Vaccine): Provides immunity against polio, a highly infectious viral disease that can cause paralysis.

6 weeks

  • DTwP /DTaP1, Hib-1, IPV-1, Hep B2, PCV 1, Rota-1

DTwP / DTaP (Diphtheria, Tetanus, Pertussis Vaccine - 1st dose): Protects against diphtheria, tetanus, and whooping cough (pertussis), serious infectious diseases caused by bacteria.

Hib-1 (Haemophilus influenzae type b Vaccine - 1st dose): Prevents Haemophilus influenzae type b infections, which can cause serious illnesses like meningitis and pneumonia.

IPV-1 (Inactivated Polio Vaccine - 1st dose): Provides immunity against polio, a highly infectious viral disease that can cause paralysis.

Hep B2 (Hepatitis B Vaccine 2nd dose): Continues to build immunity against hepatitis B, a serious liver infection caused by the hepatitis B virus.

PCV 1 (Pneumococcal Conjugate Vaccine - 1st dose): Protects against pneumococcal diseases such as pneumonia, meningitis, and bloodstream infections caused by Streptococcus pneumoniae bacteria.

Rota-1 (Rotavirus Vaccine - 1st dose): Prevents rotavirus infections, a common cause of severe diarrhea and dehydration in infants and young children.

10 weeks

  • DTwP /DTaP2, Hib-2, IPV-2, Hep B3, PCV 2, Rota-2

DTwP / DTaP (Diphtheria, Tetanus, Pertussis Vaccine - 2nd dose): Boosts immunity against diphtheria, tetanus, and whooping cough (pertussis).

Hib-2 (Haemophilus influenzae type b Vaccine - 2nd dose): Provides additional protection against Haemophilus influenzae type b infections.

IPV-2 (Inactivated Polio Vaccine - 2nd dose): Boosts immunity against polio.

Hep B3 (Hepatitis B Vaccine 3rd dose): Further strengthens immunity against hepatitis B.

PCV 2 (Pneumococcal Conjugate Vaccine - 2nd dose): Provides continued protection against pneumococcal diseases.

Rota-2 (Rotavirus Vaccine - 2nd dose): Reinforces protection against rotavirus infections.

14 weeks

  • DTwP /DTaP3, Hib-3, IPV-3, Hep B4, PCV 3, Rota-3*

DTwP / DTaP (Diphtheria, Tetanus, Pertussis Vaccine - 3rd dose): Completes the primary series for diphtheria, tetanus, and whooping cough (pertussis) vaccinations.

Hib-3 (Haemophilus influenzae type b Vaccine - 3rd dose): Completes the primary series for Haemophilus influenzae type b vaccinations.

IPV-3 (Inactivated Polio Vaccine - 3rd dose): Completes the primary series for inactivated polio vaccinations.

Hep B4 (Hepatitis B Vaccine 4th dose): Provides the final dose to ensure long-term immunity against hepatitis B.

PCV 3 (Pneumococcal Conjugate Vaccine - 3rd dose): Completes the primary series for pneumococcal conjugate vaccinations.

Rota-3 (Rotavirus Vaccine - 3rd dose): Provides the final dose for full protection against rotavirus infections.

6 months

  • Influenza-1

Influenza Vaccine (Flu Shot - 1st dose): Provides protection against influenza viruses, which can cause seasonal flu outbreaks.

7 months

  • Influenza-2

Influenza Vaccine (Flu Shot - 2nd dose): Continues to provide protection against influenza viruses.

6-9 months

  • Typhoid Conjugate Vaccine

Typhoid Conjugate Vaccine: Protects against typhoid fever, a bacterial infection caused by Salmonella Typhi bacteria, commonly found in contaminated food and water.

9 months

  • MMR 1 (Mumps, Measles, Rubella)

MMR Vaccine (Measles, Mumps, Rubella - 1st dose): Protects against measles, mumps, and rubella, three highly contagious viral diseases that can cause serious complications.

12 months

  • Hepatitis A- 1

Hepatitis A Vaccine (1st dose): Prevents hepatitis A, a viral liver disease usually spread through contaminated food and water.

12 - 15 months

  • PCV Booster

PCV Booster (Pneumococcal Conjugate Vaccine Booster): Provides a booster dose to reinforce immunity against pneumococcal diseases.

15 months

  • MMR 2, Varicella

MMR Vaccine (Measles, Mumps, Rubella - 2nd dose): Completes the MMR vaccine series for full protection against measles, mumps, and rubella.

Varicella Vaccine (Chickenpox Vaccine): Prevents chickenpox, a highly contagious viral infection caused by the varicella-zoster virus.

16 - 18 months

  • DTwP /DTaP, Hib, IPV

DTwP / DTaP (Diphtheria, Tetanus, Pertussis Vaccine - Booster): Boosts immunity against diphtheria, tetanus, and whooping cough (pertussis).

Hib (Haemophilus influenzae type b Vaccine - Booster): Boosts immunity against Haemophilus influenzae type b infections.

IPV (Inactivated Polio Vaccine - Booster): Provides a booster dose to reinforce immunity against polio.

4 – 6 years

  • DTwP /DTaP, IPV, MMR 3

DTwP / DTaP (Diphtheria, Tetanus, Pertussis Vaccine - Booster): Boosts immunity against diphtheria, tetanus, and whooping cough (pertussis).

IPV (Inactivated Polio Vaccine - Booster): Provides a booster dose to reinforce immunity against polio.

MMR Vaccine (Measles, Mumps, Rubella - Booster): Boosts immunity against measles, mumps, and rubella.
